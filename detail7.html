Unit Converter App

Unit Converter - Android Basics in Kotlin

The project aimed to build a unit converter app based on the Tip Calculator app. This app focuses on converting measurements between centimeters and inches.

This Unit Converter app serves as an introduction to theme and material design in Android. By leveraging the foundational structure of the Tip Calculator app from the Android Basics in Kotlin course, this project expanded the application's capabilities to perform unit conversions.

  1. Explored Android's theme and material design principles.
  2. Implemented conversion logic between centimeters and inches.

Check out the app's deliverable on GitHub.

Key Takeaways

Throughout this project, I acquired a deeper understanding of Android development, particularly in building intuitive user interfaces with Kotlin. The integration of theme and material design added a layer of sophistication to the app, making it user-friendly and visually appealing.

  • Practical implementation of unit conversion logic.
  • Understanding and applying material design guidelines for Android apps.
