App Development 391

Android App based on Google's Doggler App Project

The objective of App Development 391 was to develop an Android app inspired by Google's Doggler App project.

We delved into the intricacies of Android development, utilizing various tools and libraries provided by Google to create a functional and visually appealing application.

  1. Introduction to Android Studio and its features.
  2. Implementation of Google libraries and tools.

What I learned
